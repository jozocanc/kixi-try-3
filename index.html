Our Beginning

Kixi Café was born in 2020 with a simple belief: mornings should feel magical. We opened our doors in the heart of Mizner Park, Boca Raton, during one of the most uncertain times in recent memory — and the community showed up for us in ways we'll never forget.

From day one, we partnered with Wells Coffee, Boca's beloved local roaster, because we believe great coffee starts with relationships. Every cup we pull is a nod to that partnership and to the farmers and makers behind every bean.

Our Japanese soufflé pancakes became our calling card almost immediately — light, pillowy, deeply satisfying. They embody everything Kixi stands for: a little unexpected, a lot of joy, and worth every moment.

Today, Kixi is more than a café. We're a community gathering place — a dog-friendly garden where neighbors become regulars, and regulars become family. We're proud to be Boca Raton's most Instagrammable spot, and even prouder of the warmth that keeps people coming back.
